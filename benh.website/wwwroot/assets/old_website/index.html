ABOUT BEN HOLLAND

General

That's me! I'm going by Ben H on this site because it makes the URL shorter, and maybe is more memorable, but my full name is Ben Holland. Hi!

I'm a sophomore computer science major at Kent State University. I was raised in Medina Ohio, and the north-east Ohio community means a lot to me, especially the DIY music scene and the tech industry.

Code

I've always loved making things, especially pretty/unique things and interactive things. Check out my Projects page to see the bulk of the stuff I've done, or check out my resume if you want to see what I know/my formal experience.

If you're looking to hire a programmer/designer, reach out to me!! I love this stuff and would love nothing more than to make my living doing it.

Music

I have played the drums for 8+ years-- two of which were in my current band, The Drawing Blanks. I also play guitar, bass, and sing. Music theory is a big passion of mine- I only ever took one formal music theory class, but learned plenty from making dumb 8-bit songs on Garageband, and learning from my extremely talented friends.
